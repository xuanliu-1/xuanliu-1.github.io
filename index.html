Greeting and welcome to my website 😀

I am a Research Fellow in the Department of Land Economy, the University of Cambridge.

My research connects semantic data integration with urban energy transition and decision support. I am particularly interested in urban energy transitions across different spatial, sectoral, institutional, and planning contexts.

Thank you for visiting. I am always happy to exchange ideas, explore shared research interests, and develop new collaborations.
